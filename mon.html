Grading Syllabus Overview

Karate-Do is broken down into units known as Mon Grad e s, Kyu Grades and Dan Grades. Each grade contains a cross section of practice, and developed skills, with further pratice more skills are developed to proceds through the grading towards a black belt.   For junior children, the Kyu grades are further divided into Mon grades.   The grading application vary depending on the age of the candidate and further information can be obtained from the instructor.  The grading syllabus is split into a number of categories; students are expected to be proficient in each category in order to be awarded the grade.

Belt menu above links to the technical requirements for each grade.   Students over junior Yellow belt must have a current Scottish Karate Federation  licence to train and grade.  Application forms for new licences are available from your instructor, or a renewal of the licence can be made online. the licence complete with valid ocet or a copy of the email must be shown prior to grading
