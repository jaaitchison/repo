Scottish Karate-Do Federation

The Scottish Karate-Do Federation was founded in December 1970 by nine karate students, three of these founder members are still active senior members of the Scottish Karate-Do Federation today.

Hamish Adam MBE 8th Dan          Colin Clapperton 8th Dan          Allan Spence 7th Dan

The Scottish Karate-Do Federation was originally called 'Wado-Ryu Karate Committee' (Scotland). The Federation has grown to become the largest most successful karate association in Scotland with thousands of members particapating in hundreds of classes all over the central belt of Scotland.  The Scottish Karate Federation is a member of the Scottish Karate Governing Body, European Karate Federation and the World Karate Federation.

Members of the Jewel Karate Group of clubs must be members of the Scottish Karate-Do Federation to particapate in club activites, compititions and gradings.
